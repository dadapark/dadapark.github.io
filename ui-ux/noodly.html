Project Brief

Noodly was a one week long collaboration project with the developer team.The objective was building an app to solve problems that can be found in our daily life. My team chose to explore the problem spaces in dietary habits, and decided to move forward with building an app for instant noodle lovers.

What are the user needs?

Instant noodle lovers need new combinations of flavours because they are bored of the current offerings.

How might we

Help them find new recipes to try at home?
Give them the ability to experiment with their own instant Noodles?

The Goal

Developing an app that allows user to generate and share recipes which will inspire other users to experiment with their own instant noodles.
